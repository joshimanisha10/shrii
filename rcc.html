Standard Features:

Induced draft counter flow cooling tower.

Body of cooling tower is made up of RCC hence high strength.

Each type of cooling tower has a suction pope, drain and overflow pipe. Float valve will be supplied with each cooling tower. All nuts bolts, washers used in the tower are pre-galvanized for the purpose to keep it rust free through out its working life.

RCC cooling tower have longer service life since it has very negligible environmental effect.

These are most durable and can withstand high wind loads.

It has no effect of UV radiation.

Various kinds of fills and splash films can be used depending upon type of water quality used.

Fan Sections can be used of FRP or Cast aluminum alloys.

No need of additional support for film fills as it can rest on RCC structure.

Sr No Component Material
1 Body RCC Structure
2 Fills Honey Comb, Opti Grid or Turbo Fills, Splash Fills, V bar
3 Basin RCC / FRP
4 Drift eliminator PVC
5 Fan blades FRP / Cast Al
6 Fan Hub MSHDG/ CI
7 Drive Gearbox driven
8 hardware HDG Steel or SS (304)
9 Nozzles Polypropylene
10 Hot water distribution FRP Main Pipe, PVC branch Pipe.
